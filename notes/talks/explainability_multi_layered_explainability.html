# Explainability: Multi-layered Explanation from Algorithmic Impact Assessments in the GDPR

Item Description
Presented By Gianclaudio Malgieri
Paper Multi-layered Explanations from Algorithmic Impact Assessments in the GDPR

# Notes

  • Challenges exist in:
    • No legal consensus for explanation
    • Not always technically feasible
    • Explanation doesn't equal justification
  • Propose 3 layers of explanation:
    • General Information
    • Group based Information
    • Individual Explanation
  • GDPR introduces a risk based process
    • The higher the risk the stronger the safegaurds & level of explainability & transperency required
